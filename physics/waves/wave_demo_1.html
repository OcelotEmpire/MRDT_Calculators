Transverse and Longitudinal Waves

Another way to classify waves is to compare what the particles in the medium are doing to the direction the wave is traveling.

  • Trnsverse Waves - the particles in the medium oscillate in a direction perpendicular to the way the wave is traveling. A good example is a wave on a string.
  • Longitudinal Waves - the particles in the medium oscillate along the same direction as the way the wave is traveling. Sound waves are longitudinal waves.
